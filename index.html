Most deliveries are carried out by our very own drivers, Monday to Friday, between 7am and 7pm. A small percentage of our deliveries are carried out by DPD, depending on whether they're coming via the warehouse in Crewe or direct from the manufacturer.









How do I change my delivery address? Add special instructions?










At the payment stage, you're given the option to insert a different delivery address to your payment address. If you need to amend the address after the order has been confirmed, then you can send us a message via eBay.










Do I have to be in to sign for my delivery?











Normally yes, all deliveries must be signed for, at the delivery address, by a person aged 16 or above but at the moment we are following government guidelines on social distancing and will arrange this with you on the day of your delivery.










Where can I get updates on delivery time?










We will send tracking details for you to keep up to date with your delivery or get in touch with any questions and we can help you out.
